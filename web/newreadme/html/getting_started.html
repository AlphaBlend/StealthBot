Getting Started with StealthBot

I've broken this page down into two sections: initial setup in Setting Up Us the Bot and getting your bot to listen to you in Adding Yourself to the Bot. Enjoy!

Setting Up Us the Bot

When you fire up your bot, you'll see a series of menus at the top. Click on the Settings menu and select Bot Settings. The settings screen will appear. Click the button labeled Step-by-Step Configuration and follow the prompts to fill out the connection portion of setup.

If you'd like, browse through some of the other settings panels on the left and see if any other options need changing. Otherwise, your bot is ready to go; click Apply and Close, then click the Connect menu, and you're ready to go!

Adding Yourself to the Bot

Here's the deal: The bot can't read your mind. It just compares letters and numbers and decides whether or not you are who you say you are.

So, how do you get it to recognize your in-game account so that it listens to what you tell it to do?

The easiest way to go about this is to do the following:

  1. Click in the text box where you would type to talk to people on Battle.net.
  2. Type (without quotes): "/add"
  3. Hit the spacebar
  4. Double-click on your name in the channel list on the right side of the bot
  5. Hit the spacebar again, then type (without quotes): "999"
  6. Press ENTER.
After following those steps, you should be able to use any commands listed in the other section of this readme.
